Wedding Assembly Instructions

1.

Travel to Austin

Austin-Bergstrom International Airport (AUS) is the closest airport, found in the Capitol of the Greatest Country in the World. From there, our wedding venue is in range of Uber, Lyft, etc. Airport tip: If you need to eat something at AUS, try The Peached Tortilla for some Asian-influenced Tex-Mex.

2.

Stay in Austin

For overnight accommodations, have a look in the Lakeway or Four Points neighborhoods of Austin. Hotels like this Holiday Inn Express and this Residence Inn (if you need more space) are within 15 minutes of the venue. We’d be remiss to forget to mention the delightful, family-owned Drury Inn (although it’s further from the venue).

3.

Get dressed

Wear something comfortable, dressy casual! It'll be outdoors (November in Austin is generally mild, but we'll have heat lamps if things get too chilly by Texas standards).

4.

Come celebrate!

See you at Hudson Bend Ranch at 5102 Doss Rd, Austin, TX 78734!
